## Provider Posture

- Mercury has full current API-family descriptor coverage in the provider
  operation registry.
- Mercury production live execution is limited to the explicit read-only
  allowlist: accounts, balances, organization-wide cards, and
  organization-wide transactions.
- Mercury money movement, card creation, card update, and card lifecycle
  operations remain non-executable provider-operation plans or local intent
  envelopes until a later approval/idempotency/conformance gate enables them.
- Erste BCR is modeled as PSD2 AIS/PIS conformance-only coverage using BCR,
  Erste Group, and Berlin Group public documentation.
- Erste BCR live execution is disabled until registered TPP portal access,
  bank-specific credentials, certificate-backed transport, consent, and SCA
  flows are verified.
